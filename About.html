About Weather App

Welcome to the Weather App! This app provides you with real-time weather information for various cities around the world. Stay informed about the current temperature, cloud percentage, wind speed, and more.

Features:

  • Real-time weather data retrieval from a reliable API.
  • User-friendly interface for easy navigation.
  • Accurate and up-to-date information for any city you choose.

How to Use:

Simply enter the name of the city you want to check the weather for in the search bar. Press the "Submit" button, and you'll receive detailed weather information for the specified location.
